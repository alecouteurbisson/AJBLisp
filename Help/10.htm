(caar <list>) ... (cddr <list>), (caaar <list>) ... (cdddr <list>)

Function

These functions return the result of successive applications of car or cdr. Each a or d in the name represents the application of car or cdr respectively.

The nth and nthcdr functions are usually clearer when they suffice.



Examples: 

> (cadar '((1 2 3) 4 (5 6)))
= 2   ; (car (cdr (car '((1 2 3) 4 (5 6)))))

> (caddr '((1 2 3) 4 (5 6)))
= 5   ; (car (cdr (cdr '((1 2 3) 4 (5 6)))))
