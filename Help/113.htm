(eql <this> <that>)

Function

Compares two lists or vectors for identity or any other objects for equality of type and value.



Examples: 

> (eql (concat "Hi " "World!") "Hi World!")
= t

> (eql  '(a b c) '(a b c))
= nil                  ; Equal but not identical

> (eql 1 1)
= t                    ; Equal integers

> (eql 1 1.0)
= nil                  ; Different types
