(case <expr> (<match> <body>...) ... [(:else <body>...)])

Macro

Evaluate <expr> and search for an eql <match> in the clauses that follow. <match> may be an atom or a list of atoms, any of which may match with <expr>. The <body>... of the first matching clause is evaluated (like progn) and returned. If the matching process reaches a clause beginning with :else then this automatically matches. There may be clauses following the :else clause but these will always be ignored.

Failure to locate a match results in an error.
