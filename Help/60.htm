(string <any>)

Function

Convert numbers and symbols to strings. Will also convert a character or a list or vector of characters to a string.

A list argument must be a flat list of characters only.

Vectors of characters will be converted up to the first nil. The vectors content after the first nil is irrelevant but all prior items must be characters. If there is no nil marker then the entire vector will be converted.



Examples: 

> (string 123)
= "123"          ; depends on integer-format setting

> (string #(#\a #\b #\c nil #\d wibble))
= "abc"
