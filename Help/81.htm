(encode-time <y> <m> <d> [<H> <M> <S>])

Function

Encodes a date or date and time as an integer time. The time is always given as a standard time rather than as DST because DST can be ambiguous. The time may be omitted and will default to 00:00:00.

The allowable range of calendar times is Jan 1 1970 00:00:00 to Jan 19 2038 03:14:07 UTC.
