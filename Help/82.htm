(date-time [<itime>])

Function

Returns a list of (year month day hours minutes seconds wday yday dst) for the integer time or for the current time if the argument is omitted. wday is the weekday (0-6, 0=>Sun), yday is the year day (0-355) and dst is a boolean which is T if daylight savings time is in effect.

The allowable range of calendar times is Jan 1 1970 00:00:00 to Jan 19 2038 03:14:07 UTC.



Examples: 

> (date-time 333333333)
= (1980 7 25 1 35 33 5 206 t)
